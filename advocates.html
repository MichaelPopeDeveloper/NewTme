MAXIMUM PARTICIPATION

Social and Economic Advocate Membership Levels are Designed for MAXIMUM Participation. Our Membership Foundation is based on the 2 BIGGEST ADVANTAGES of Every Disenfranchised Community.

The Micro Economic Spending Power of the MASSIVE Number of People Affected in Communities Across the Country.

As Participation INCREASES on ANY Level, the Immediate IMPACT is Revenue Stream Developement, New Entrepreneurs, Initial Infrastructure and More!

All Advocate Members can Develop Uber Style Compensation for 3 mo., 6 mo., or Long Term just for Sharing Information!

RAPID RESPONSE

Social Advocates are Dedicated Servants that want to take their “Participatory Value” in the Social Arena to the Next Level.

They want to be on the Front Lines Working Directly with People in Need. (volunteers, advisory/planning committees, non-profits, etc).

The Need is so Massive and GROWING at such a Rapid Pace, that Tens of Thousands of Social Advocates can be Developed Across the Country. This group can be EQUIPPED with the Resources to Serve this Tremendous Social Crisis through Direct Services at the Grassroots Level.

IN THE TRENCHES

Social Advocates can Develop Uber Style Revenue Streams for Themselves/Organization while Helping to Create Numerous Social Services Vital to a Successful Recovery of this population.

While Social Advocates Are Economic Advocates as well, their Main Focus is Fighting “In the Trenches” with the Ones They Serve. Assisting them to Select from among the many Options Available to Improve their Situation.
